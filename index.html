About Us

Our Story

What started as a dream sketched on a napkin in 2019 has become the neighborhood gathering place we always imagined. We’re more than just coffee. We’re the morning ritual that starts your day right, the afternoon escape when you need a moment, and the cozy corner where friends become family.

From our ethically sourced beans to our pastries baked fresh every morning at 4 AM, everything we create is made with intention and care. We believe in supporting the farmers we partner with, using local ingredients whenever possible, and creating a space where everyone feels welcome.

Every cup we pour and every pastry we bake comes from our belief that great coffee should bring people together. Welcome to our table.
